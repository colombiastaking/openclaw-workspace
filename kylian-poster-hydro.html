💧 THE DAM

Water Makes Electricity!

⚡

📖 What is it?

  • A dam is a big wall built across a river.
  • It stops water and makes a lake behind it.
  • Water flows through the dam and spins wheels.
  • The spinning wheels make electricity for our homes!
🌧️ Rain fills the lake
🌊 Water flows down
⚙️ Wheels spin fast
💡 We get light!

💡 Did You Know?

The biggest dam in the world is in China — it's called the Three Gorges Dam. It's as long as 500 soccer fields! The water can fill 4,000 swimming pools every second!
