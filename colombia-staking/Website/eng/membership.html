Colombia Staking is introducing COLS token: scarce,  community driven and with long term incentives for delegators.


30% of agency income is used to buy COLS: 


 —> 1/3 to COLS staked at our decentralized organization. (Daily rewards proportional to amount of staked COLS in the DAO)

—> 2/3 to people with eGLD delegated at Colombia Staking and with staked COLS.(daily rewards)






The rewards at the DAO are distributed via the Peerme protocol, there is a minimum of 15 days lockup when the COLS are staked. The COLS can be unstaked after the 15 days.  


The rewards for eGLD delegators are sent daily directly to their address. The more eGLD delegated, the more rewards and the higher your ratio COLS/eGLD is, the better the APR bonus is for  your delegated eGLD at Colombia Staking.



Utilities of the COLS token are

   1. Vote and propose at Colombia Staking DAO on PeerMe.

Gold Member 

Benefit: 0% service fee in the agency*.

-NFT link: COL-70965c

-Official market places: 

-Xoxno.com

-Telegram channel Gold Members (APR, rewards, info) https://t.me/Colombia_Staking_Gold_Member

The first 50 delegators of Colombia Staking were given a Gold Member NFT, which gives them 0% service fee for their eGLD delegated* at Colombia Staking. Every 2 weeks, the fees collected by the agency are redistributed to the NFT holders according to their proportion of eGLD staked in Colombia Staking pool.


If the NFT is transferred to another account or sold, the service fee benefit is also transferred. 


Example: I own a Gold Member NFT and I staked 50 eGLD to Colombia Staking. The agency collected 5 eGLD of fees during the week and has a total staked eGLD of 5000. You will get back for the week: 50/5000*5=0,05 eGLD

*A maximum amount of 500 staked eGLD per NFT is eligible for the 0% service fee.
