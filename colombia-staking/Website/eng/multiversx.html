What is MultiversX?

MultiversX (formerly Elrond) is a highly scalable, secure, and decentralized blockchain network designed to power the future of digital finance and Web3 applications. Using Adaptive State Sharding and Secure Proof of Stake (SPoS) consensus, MultiversX achieves unprecedented transaction throughput while maintaining decentralization and security.
