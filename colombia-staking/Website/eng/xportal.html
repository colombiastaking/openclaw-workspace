What is Xportal?

Xportal is the official mobile and browser wallet for MultiversX. It's a "Super App" that combines cryptocurrency wallet functionality with Web3 features, social capabilities, and access to the broader MultiversX ecosystem. With Xportal, you can securely store your eGLD, interact with dApps, stake your tokens, and much more.

🔐

Secure Storage

Your private keys are encrypted and stored locally on your device. Only you have access to your funds.

⚡

🎮

Web3 Access

Connect to dApps, NFT marketplaces, DeFi platforms, and games built on MultiversX.

Stake your eGLD directly from the app with just a few taps. No technical knowledge required.

How to Get Started

1

Download Xportal

Get Xportal from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). You can also use the browser extension for desktop.

2

Create or Import Wallet

Follow the setup wizard to create a new wallet. Write down your secret phrase and store it safely - this is your only backup!

3

Buy or Transfer eGLD

Purchase eGLD from an exchange and transfer it to your Xportal wallet address, or use the built-in fiat on-ramp.

4

Stake with Colombia Staking

Open the staking section in Xportal or visit our dApp to delegate your eGLD to Colombia Staking.
