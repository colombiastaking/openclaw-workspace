### Gold Member APR Formula (CORRECTED)
- Base APR from API: ~8.41% (with 10% fee deducted)
- Raw APR (before fee): baseApr / 0.9 = ~9.35%
- Gold Bonus = Raw APR × Service Fee × min(1, capacity/userEgld)
- Example: 500 eGLD → +0.94% bonus → effective APR 9.35%
- Example: 1000 eGLD (1 NFT) → +0.47% bonus → effective APR 8.88%
